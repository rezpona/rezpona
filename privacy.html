01Who we are

Rezpona provides software that helps restaurants, hotels, cafés, and other hospitality businesses analyze and respond to their online reviews. For the purposes of GDPR, Rezpona acts as a data controller for account and website data, and as a data processor when handling review data on behalf of our business customers.

The Service is operated by Rezpona. You can contact us about any privacy matter at:

  • Email: rezpona@gmail.com
  • Alternative email: rezpona@outlook.com

02Information we collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, business name, business type, and login credentials.

Google Business Profile data

If you connect your Google Business Profile, we access your business reviews, ratings, and related metadata so we can analyze them and help you draft replies. This is done through Google's official, authorized API using OAuth — we never see or store your Google password.

Payment information

Subscription payments are processed by our payment provider (Stripe). We do not store your full card details on our servers; we only retain limited billing metadata such as your plan, billing status, and the last four digits of your card.

Usage & technical data

We collect information about how you use the Service, such as feature usage, log data, device and browser type, and IP address, to operate, secure, and improve the platform.

03How we use your data

  • To provide, maintain, and operate the Service.
  • To analyze your reviews and generate suggested reply drafts.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription.
  • To send you service-related communications (e.g., review alerts, account notices).
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or abuse.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your review content to train publicly available AI models.

04Google user data

Rezpona's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We only access your Google Business Profile review data to provide the review-management features you have requested. We do not use this data for advertising, do not sell it, and do not share it with third parties except the sub-processors strictly necessary to deliver the Service. You can revoke Rezpona's access at any time from your Google Account settings.

05Legal bases for processing

Under the GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract — to provide the Service you have signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — to secure, improve, and support the Service.
  • Consent — where required, for example when you connect your Google account or opt in to marketing.
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable laws (e.g., tax and accounting).

06Sharing & sub-processors

We share data only with trusted service providers who help us run the Service, under agreements that require them to protect your data:

  • Cloud hosting & infrastructure — to store and serve data securely.
  • Payment processing — Stripe, to handle subscriptions.
  • AI processing — to analyze reviews and generate reply drafts. Review text may be sent to our AI provider solely to produce your draft; it is not used to train their public models.
  • Google — to read reviews and publish your approved replies via the official API.
  • Communication tools — to send you alerts and account emails.

We may also disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

07Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. After you close your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it for legal, tax, or security reasons.

08Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and OAuth-based authentication for third-party connections. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work continuously to safeguard your information.

09Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Request a portable copy of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at rezpona@gmail.com.

10Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and to operate the Service, and limited analytics cookies to understand usage and improve the product. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling essential cookies may affect functionality.

11International data transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, including outside the European Economic Area. Where this happens, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses to ensure your data remains protected.

12Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and revise the "Last updated" date. For material changes, we will provide additional notice where appropriate.

13Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: rezpona@gmail.com
  • Alternative email: rezpona@outlook.com

We aim to respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.
